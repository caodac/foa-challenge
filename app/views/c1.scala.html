Welcome to the NCATS Translator Challenge! This is a unique URL that you may wish to share with fellow team members. There are six tasks to be completed. Each task should take anywhere from minutes, to over an hour to complete. Tasks must be completed in sequence. If you leave the site before completing all of the tasks, you will return where you left off. However, for tasks that have multiple components, leaving the site prior to completing all components may result in loss of your entries and require you to re-enter the information upon returning to the site. There is no limit to the number of times you may try to answer each task.

We will not be able to provide additional hints for the tasks. Upon successful completion of each task, you will be provided with access to a portion of the FOA. The FOA link is available in the top navigation panel.

NCATS takes this program and the review process very seriously. However, there are elements of humor injected in some of the challenge tasks. This lightheartedness is intended to be engaging and should not be misconstrued as a lack of appreciation of the obstacles to be overcome to construct a reasoning tool, or the importance of the application and review process itself.
