Knowledge representation is a fundamental challenge in artificial intelligence (AI) and automated reasoning. The challenge, to a large extent, is precipitated by the inherent lack of unambiguous semantics and lexicon needed to translate data into knowledge. Such translation often requires sufficient knowledge of basic graph theory and algorithms.
